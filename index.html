Welcome to Christ Cathedral Apostolic Church — a thriving apostolic ministry in the heart of Baltimore with a passion to see lives transformed by the power of Jesus Christ. We are a church committed to preaching truth, building strong disciples, and creating an atmosphere where people can encounter God in a real and life-changing way.

At Christ Cathedral, our mission is to reach souls, restore families, and raise up believers who are rooted in the Word of God and empowered by the Holy Ghost. We believe that church is more than a service — it is a community of faith where people from every background can grow spiritually, discover purpose, and become everything God has called them to be.

Under the leadership of Bishop Dr. Justin Marcus and First Lady Brandi Marcus, Christ Cathedral is known for powerful worship, practical biblical teaching, intentional discipleship, and a heart for outreach. From dynamic worship to evangelism, prayer, leadership development, and community impact — our goal is to equip people to live victorious lives both inside and outside the church.

"Whether you are new to church, returning to your faith, or looking for a place to grow deeper in God — Christ Cathedral is a place where you can belong, be loved, and be transformed."

What to Expect

When you arrive, you will be greeted by friendly people ready to help you feel comfortable and connected. Our worship experiences are energetic, spirit-filled, and centered around the Word of God.

Dynamic Worship
Spirit-filled praise and prayer that ushers you into God's presence
Biblical Teaching
Powerful, practical preaching rooted in the Word of God
Warm Community
A welcoming, family-oriented atmosphere where everyone belongs
For Families
Safe, engaging environments for children, youth, and adults
Arrival: We encourage you to arrive 15–20 minutes early to park, meet our team, and get settled.

Dress: Casual or Sunday best — you are welcome here.

After Your Visit: Introduce yourself to one of our leaders — we would love to stay connected and serve your family.
